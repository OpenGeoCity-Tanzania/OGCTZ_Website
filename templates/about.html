About OpenGeoCity

We are pioneering geospatial innovation and data-driven urban strategy to build smarter, more resilient cities in Tanzania.

Our Mission

We bridge the gap between traditional urban planning and modern data science to empower cities with geospatial insights. Our mission is to democratize access to geographic data and geospatial intelligence.

We believe that informed decisions based on accurate spatial data can transform cities, improve lives, and create a more sustainable future for Tanzania.

Our Vision

To build a Tanzania where every city and community leverages geospatial innovation to create inclusive, resilient, and sustainable urban environments.

"Data Drives Development. Geography Guides Growth."
