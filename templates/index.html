Who We Are

A Geospatial Intelligence Lab
for Tanzania's Future

At OpenGeoCity Tanzania, we firmly believe that maps are the intelligence that shapes a better world — not merely a collection of lines and colors.

Geospatial Intelligence

Rooted in the art and science of mapping, we create accurate, actionable spatial data that empowers diverse stakeholders — from governments to local communities — to make well-informed decisions.

Smart Urban Planning

We leverage cutting-edge geospatial tools to visualize, analyze, and solve complex spatial issues — driving urbanization, environmental management, and sustainable development across Tanzania.

Capacity Building

Beyond mapping, we are a laboratory of ideas — offering training programs that equip urban practitioners and graduates with essential geospatial skills, empowering them to contribute to sustainable development.
