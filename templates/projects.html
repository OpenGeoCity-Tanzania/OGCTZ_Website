Impact Wallet

Impact Wallet is a field intelligence platform built for Tanzania — combining real-time geospatial data collection, partner API integrations, and a dedicated Android app to measure and track on-the-ground social and environmental impact.

The platform empowers organizations, NGOs, and government agencies to deploy field agents, capture geo-tagged data points, and generate actionable impact reports — bridging the gap between field operations and strategic decision-making aligned with Tanzania's sustainable development goals.
